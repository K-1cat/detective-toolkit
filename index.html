Who Ate the Cookies?

Case File & Encrypted Evidence Decoder

The Incident

Last night, someone stole a large amount of cookies from the living room jar.
The thief left encrypted evidence and tricky riddles all over the house.

Your mission: Find the evidence, decode the messages, solve the riddles, and identify the culprit.

Suspect Profiles

Brother Kanoa

Your brother who games late at night. Loves snacks and knows every corner of the house.

Mr. Shadow

A quiet neighbor who moves like a shadow. Seen near the kitchen at night and secretly loves sweets.

Ms. Sweettooth

A friend with a spare key who always praises your cookies. Has an obvious sweet tooth.

Captain Crumb

An eccentric man who leaves crumbs everywhere. He was in the house that night telling sailing stories.

Lady Midnight

A mysterious woman active only at night. Loves secretive adventures and was heard near the piano.

Professor Riddle

A puzzle-loving professor who created many of the encrypted clues. He denies eating any cookies.

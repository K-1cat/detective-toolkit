The Incident

Last night, someone broke into the living room cookie jar and stole a tons of cookies. The thief left behind a trail of encrypted evidence scattered throughout the house. We have six suspects. Each has a motive, opportunity, and suspicious habits. Your mission is to find the hidden evidence, decode the messages, solve the riddles, and piece together the truth.

Suspect Profiles

Brother Kanoa

Motive: Loves late-night snacks and has easy access to the cookie jar. Opportunity: Was home all night. Alibi: Claims he was playing online games with friends until morning.

Mr. Shadow

Motive: Has a secret sweet tooth but never admits it. Opportunity: Was seen near the living room window that night. Alibi: Says he was taking a night walk and didn’t enter the house.

Ms. Sweettooth

Motive: Obsessed with sweets; always asks for extra cookies. Opportunity: Visited the house earlier that evening. Alibi: Claims she went straight home after dinner.

Captain Crumb

Motive: Extreme love for cookies. Opportunity: Was in the house that night. Alibi: Says he was asleep in the guest room, but his stories keep changing.

Lady Midnight

Motive: Enjoys the thrill of midnight adventures. Opportunity: Was humming near the piano around midnight. Alibi: Claims she was writing poetry under the moonlight outside.

Professor Riddle

Motive: None for the theft itself, but loves riddles. Opportunity: Visited the house and was experimenting with the globe. Alibi: He was “creating intellectual challenges” for fun and denies any involvement in stealing.

The Incident

Last night, someone broke into the living room cookie jar and stole a tons of cookies. The thief left behind a trail of encrypted evidence scattered throughout the house. We have six suspects. Each has a motive, opportunity, and suspicious habits. Your mission is to find the hidden evidence, decode the messages, solve the riddles, and piece together the truth.

Suspect Profiles

Brother Kanoa

Your brother who games late at night. Loves snacks and knows every corner of the house.

Mr. Shadow

A quiet neighbor who moves like a shadow. Seen near the kitchen at night and secretly loves sweets.

Ms. Sweettooth

A friend with a spare key who always praises your cookies. Has an obvious sweet tooth.

Captain Crumb

An eccentric man who leaves crumbs everywhere and calls cookies “treasure of the seas.” He was in the house that night telling sailing stories.

Lady Midnight

A mysterious woman active only at night. Loves secretive adventures and was heard near the piano.

Professor Riddle

A puzzle-loving professor who created many of the encrypted clues “because it would be more fun.” Seen by the calender, but he denies eating any cookies.
